# Vita Press Label Design

**Date**: 2026-03-04
**Format**: 70mm x 100mm self-adhesive rectangular labels for 250ml, 330ml, and 500ml clear PET bottles
**Approach**: Clean minimal (Approach A) — white background, coloured blend name, juice colour visible through clear bottle

---

## Layout

- **Top**: VITA PRESS wordmark (Instrument Serif, 8pt, letter-spacing 0.15em, uppercase, charcoal)
- **Top edge**: 2px accent line in blend colour
- **Middle**: Blend name (Instrument Serif, 22pt, blend accent colour), ingredient dots below (DM Sans Light, 7pt)
- **Separator**: Thin dashed line
- **Tagline**: "Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food" (DM Sans Italic, 5.5pt, muted)
- **Bottom**: Legal text block (DM Sans Regular, 5.5pt, muted) + QR code (12mm square, bottom-right)

## Typography

- Wordmark: Instrument Serif, 8pt, letter-spacing 0.15em
- Blend name: Instrument Serif, 22pt
- Ingredients: DM Sans Light 300, 7pt
- Tagline: DM Sans Italic 300, 6pt
- Legal: DM Sans Regular 400, 5.5pt

## Deliverable

Single HTML/CSS file containing 11 label variants: 5 adult blends at 330ml, 5 adult blends at 500ml, 1 kid blend (Sunshine) at 250ml. All print-ready at 70mm x 100mm, exportable to PDF via browser print.
