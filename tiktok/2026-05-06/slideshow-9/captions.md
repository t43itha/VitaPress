## FULL CAPTION
Hot take: Innocent smoothies are basically dessert 🍬

I'll probably get dragged for this but someone needs to say it. Innocent smoothies market themselves as healthy but one bottle has more sugar than a Krispy Kreme donut. I'm not even exaggerating.

They blend fruit with concentrates and it spikes your blood sugar like mad. You feel energised for 20 minutes then crash HARD. I used to drink one every morning and wonder why I was starving by 10am. It's because it's basically liquid sugar.

A proper cold-pressed juice uses whole fruits and veg with no added concentrates. It's got fibre, enzymes, vitamins that your body can actually use. It doesn't taste like a milkshake and that's the POINT. Real food isn't supposed to taste like dessert.

I'm not saying never have an Innocent smoothie. I'm just saying call it what it is — a treat, not a health drink. If you want actual nutrition, get something that hasn't been blended with apple concentrate and marketed with cartoon fruit on the bottle.
